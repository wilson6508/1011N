printf = format and print data
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
%-3s

%: 開頭

-: 左對齊(不寫為右對齊)
3: 佔3個字符

s: string
c: character
d: decimal
f: floating point
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
printf "%-3s %2d %2d %2d\n" "xm" "11" "12" "13" && printf "%-3s %2d %2d %2d\n" "xmm" "22" "23" "24"
